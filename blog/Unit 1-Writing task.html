學名: Ursus arctos horribilis

There are grizzlies in Yellowstone National Park and Grand Teton National Park.

在黃石國家公園和大提頓國家公園也有灰熊棲息。

Grizzlies often live alone, but in the coastal areas, the Grizzlies gather in streams, rivers, lakes and ponds in the season of salmon spawning.
Female Grizzlies produce 1 to 4 cubs every other year. The weight of each young child is only 500 grams.

Female Grizzlies weighing 130 to 200 kg, the average weight of male Grizzlies 180 to 360 kg, the average length of subspecies 198 cm.
Running at speeds of up to 40 kilometers per hour.

The Grizzlies in British Columbia have been up to 25,000 but the number has fallen significantly due to hunting and habitat loss.
In 2008, the number of Grizzlies is estimated at 16014, the current number of Grizzlies in North America is about 55,000.

在2008年,灰熊的數量估計為16014隻,目前北美洲的灰熊數量大約為55000隻。
